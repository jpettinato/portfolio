About

I'm a private pilot and aviation student at The Ohio State University, working towards a career in commercial aviation. Along with my involvement as the Student Executive Officer of EAA Chapter 283, I'm committed to helping inspire and grow the next generation of aviators. I've helped to build an aviation program at my high school that connects students with industry professionals and allows them opportunities such as seeing numerous aviation professions first-hand or having access to local, discounted flight training. Through the EAA, I also work to introduce local youth to flight at Young Eagles flights and kit-plane build sessions. Beyond the cockpit, I channel my interest in technology into personal projects, including my current undertaking to build a thrust-vectored rocket designed for propulsive landing.
